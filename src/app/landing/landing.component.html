Mitchell-Lama income affidavit

Brief description about the form

The deadline for residents to file is now extended to May 17, 2021. Managing agents will calculate surcharges based on the 2020 Income Affidavits that will take effect August 1, 2021. Current surcharges remain in effect until July 31, 2021. As a reminder, Income Affidavits do not have to be notarized this year
